LIC Policy Premium Payment Online Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Official Guide

LIC policy ka premium online pay karna ab bahut easy aur safe ho gaya hai. Agar aap apna policy premium due hai aur aapko branch visit nahi karna hai, to ye guide step by step aapko help karegi.

LIC Policy Premium Payment Kya Hai

Premium payment ek regular payment hai jo policy ke liye required hota hai. Online premium payment ka matlab hai ki aap apne bank account ya mobile app se directly LIC ko pay karte hain, bina branch visit ke.

Required Details / Documents

  • Policy Number
  • Registered Mobile Number linked with LIC policy
  • Bank account / debit/credit card / UPI details
  • Email ID (optional for receipt)
  • LIC Mobile App (if using app method)

Step 1 – Official LIC Portal Open Kare

Browser me LIC Official Website open kare. Top menu me “Pay Premium Online” ya “e-Services” option select kare.

Step 2 – Login / Policy Details Enter Kare

“Policy Number” aur registered mobile number enter kare. OTP verify kare jo aapke mobile pe aayega.

Step 3 – Payment Method Select Kare

Available payment options me se select kare:

  • Net Banking
  • Debit / Credit Card
  • UPI Payment
  • LIC Mobile App Payment

Step 4 – Premium Amount Confirm Kare

Policy number select karne ke baad pending premium amount automatically show hoga. Amount check kare aur confirm kare.

Step 5 – OTP / Payment Verification

Bank / UPI OTP enter kare aur transaction complete kare. Payment confirmation page aayega aur receipt generate hoga.

Step 6 – Receipt Download / Save Kare

Receipt ko download kare aur save kare. Ye proof of payment hai aur future reference ke liye important hai.

LIC Mobile App Se Premium Payment

  1. LIC Mobile App install kare (Android / iOS)
  2. Login kare apne policy number aur registered mobile number se
  3. “Pay Premium” option select kare
  4. Amount verify kare aur preferred payment method choose kare
  5. OTP verify kare aur payment complete kare
  6. Receipt download kare ya mobile me save kare

Official LIC Links

Important Tips

  • Official LIC portal ya mobile app hi use kare
  • OTP aur bank details safe rakhe
  • Payment receipt download aur save kare
  • Pending premium amount timely pay kare taaki policy lapse na ho
  • Multiple policies hai to individually pay kare

Benefits of Online Premium Payment

  • Branch visit ki zarurat nahi
  • Fast and secure process
  • Receipt instant generate hota hai
  • 24/7 online access from mobile or computer
  • Policy lapse se bacha ja sakta hai

Conclusion

LIC policy premium payment online karna ab easy, safe aur official process hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apne LIC policy ka premium easily pay kar sakte ho aur receipt download kar sakte ho.
