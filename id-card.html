About School ID Card Generator

The School ID Card Generator is a free online tool designed to help schools create professional identity cards for students quickly and easily. Instead of using complicated design software, teachers and administrators can simply enter student information and generate a complete identity card within seconds.

This tool allows users to upload the school logo, student photograph, and principal signature to create a realistic ID card layout similar to a real PVC school card. The watermark logo background also helps give the card a professional appearance.

How to Use

Enter the school name and student details such as name, parent name, class, section, roll number, and phone number. Upload the school logo, student photo, and principal signature. Select your preferred template color and click preview to generate the card instantly. Once the card is generated, you can download it as an image.
