FASTag Account Balance Check Karne Ka Complete Guide

FASTag India me toll payment ko easy banane ke liye use kiya jata hai. FASTag ek electronic toll collection system hai jisse aap toll plaza par bina rukhe payment kar sakte ho.

Agar aap vehicle use karte ho aur FASTag lagaya hai to aapko apna FASTag balance check karna zaruri hota hai.

FASTag Kya Hota Hai

FASTag ek RFID based sticker hota hai jo vehicle ke windshield par lagaya jata hai. Toll plaza par scanner is tag ko read karta hai aur toll amount automatically deduct ho jata hai.
