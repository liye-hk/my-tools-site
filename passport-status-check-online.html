Passport Application Status Check Karne Ka Complete Guide

Agar aapne passport ke liye apply kiya hai to aap online apna passport application status check kar sakte ho. India me passport services government ke Passport Seva portal ke through manage ki jati hain.

Passport Status Kya Hota Hai

Passport status ka matlab hai ki aapki passport application abhi kis stage par hai. Isse pata chalta hai ki passport approve hua hai ya abhi processing me hai.

  • Application received
  • Under review
  • Police verification
  • Passport printed
  • Passport dispatched

Passport Status Check Karne Ke Liye Required Details

  • Application number
  • Date of birth
  • Internet connection

Passport Status Online Kaise Check Kare

Step 1

Official passport website open kare:

Passport Seva Official Website

Step 2

Track Application Status option par click kare.

Step 3

Application type select kare (Passport/PCC/IC).

Step 4

Application number aur date of birth enter kare.

Step 5

Submit button par click kare aur passport status screen par show ho jayega.

Passport Status Check Karne Ke Benefits

  • Application progress pata chal jata hai
  • Passport dispatch status check kar sakte hain
  • Delay ka reason pata chal sakta hai

Passport Delivery Time

Normally passport apply karne ke baad 15 se 30 din ke andar passport deliver ho jata hai.
