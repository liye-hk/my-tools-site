Aadhaar PVC Card Order Online Kaise Kare

Step 1 – Official Portal Open Kare

UIDAI Official Website open kare. Yeh India government का official portal है।

Step 2 – Order Aadhaar PVC Option

"Order Aadhaar PVC Card" option select kare. Yahan se aap online request start कर सकते हैं।

Step 3 – Aadhaar Number Enter Kare

Apna 12 digit Aadhaar number aur captcha code enter kare. Make sure registered mobile number active ho क्योंकि OTP verification ke liye use होगा।

Step 4 – OTP Verification

Registered mobile number par OTP aayega. OTP enter kare aur submit kare।

Step 5 – Payment Kare

Aadhaar PVC card ke liye nominal fee ₹50 (India) hoti hai. Payment gateway select karke online payment complete kare।

Step 6 – PVC Card Delivery

Payment complete hone ke baad 5–10 working days me PVC Aadhaar card address par deliver ho jayega.
