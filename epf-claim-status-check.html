EPF Claim Status Check Kaise Kare Online 2026

PF Account Se Claim Status Verify Karne Ka Complete Guide

Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) account holders apna claim status online check kar sakte hain. Agar aapne PF withdrawal, advance ya transfer ke liye apply kiya hai, to claim status verify karna zaruri hai।

EPF Claim Status Kya Hota Hai

EPF claim status se pata chalta hai ki aapka PF withdrawal, transfer ya advance request process me kis stage par hai।

  • Application submitted
  • Under verification
  • Approved / Rejected
  • Amount credited

EPF Claim Status Check Karne Ke Liye Required Details

  • UAN number
  • Registered mobile number
  • Aadhaar linked with UAN
  • PF account details

EPF Claim Status Online Kaise Check Kare

Step 1 – Official EPF Portal Open Kare

EPF Member Portal open kare। Ye Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) ka official portal hai।

Step 2 – UAN Login Kare

Apna UAN number aur password enter kare। OTP verification bhi ho sakta hai registered mobile par।

Step 3 – Online Services Tab

“Online Services” tab me jaake “Track Claim Status” option select kare।

Step 4 – Claim Status Verify Kare

Apne application number ya request details enter kare aur submit kare। EPF claim status screen par show ho jayega।

EPF Claim Status Check Karne Ke Benefits

  • Request process track karna easy
  • Approval or rejection status pata chalega
  • Amount credited status verify kar sakte hain

Important Tips

  • Official EPF portal hi use kare
  • Registered mobile number active ho
  • UAN aur KYC details updated ho
  • Fake websites se bachke rahe
