Passport Kya Hai?

Passport ek official travel document hai jo government issue karti hai. Is document ke through aap international travel kar sakte ho aur foreign countries me apni identity prove kar sakte ho.

India me passport **Ministry of External Affairs** ke under issue hota hai.

Official website: https://www.passportindia.gov.in

Passport Ke Types

  • Ordinary Passport (Blue)
  • Official Passport (White)
  • Diplomatic Passport (Maroon)

Most people ke liye **Ordinary Passport** hi issue hota hai.

Passport Banane Ke Benefits

  • International travel

  • Strong identity proof
  • Visa apply karne ke liye zaruri

Passport Seva Portal

Step 2 New user registration kare

Portal par new account create kare.

Step 3 Login kare

Registration complete hone ke baad portal me login kare.

Step 4 Fresh Passport Apply option choose kare

Dashboard me **Apply for Fresh Passport** option select kare.

Step 5 Application form fill kare

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Address
  • Parents details
  • Education details

Step 6 Documents upload kare

Required documents upload kare jaise Aadhaar card aur address proof.

Step 7 Passport fees pay kare

Online payment karna hoga.

Normal passport fee approx **₹1500** hoti hai.

Step 8 Appointment book kare

Nearest **Passport Seva Kendra** select kare aur appointment book kare.

Step 9 PSK visit kare

Appointment date par Passport Seva Kendra visit kare.
