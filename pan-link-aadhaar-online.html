PAN Card Link with Aadhaar Online 2026

Step by Step Official Income Tax Portal Guide

PAN card ko Aadhaar ke sath online link karna ab simple aur official process ke through possible hai. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi, jaise ki e-Filing portal login, PAN-Aadhaar verification aur successful linking.

PAN Card & Aadhaar Link Kya Hai

Government ne mandate kiya hai ki PAN card ko Aadhaar ke sath link kare, warna PAN card invalid ho sakta hai. Linking ke baad tax aur other services ke liye verification fast aur official hota hai.

Required Details / Documents

  • PAN Card number
  • Aadhaar number aur registered mobile number
  • Bank account / Email ID (optional for verification)
  • Login credentials (Income Tax e-Filing portal if already registered)

Step 1 – Official Income Tax e-Filing Portal Open Kare

Browser me Income Tax e-Filing Portal open kare.

Step 2 – Link Aadhaar Option Select Kare

  • Homepage par “Link Aadhaar” option click kare
  • PAN number aur Aadhaar number enter kare
  • Name as per Aadhaar exactly match kare
  • Captcha verification complete kare

Step 3 – Submit Link Request

  • “Submit” button click kare
  • OTP registered mobile number par receive hoga
  • OTP enter karke verification complete kare
  • Successful linking acknowledgement screen me dikhega

Step 4 – Check PAN-Aadhaar Linking Status

Status check karne ke liye:

  • Official portal “Check Status” option open kare
  • PAN aur Aadhaar number enter kare
  • Linking status – Verified / Pending / Failed check kare
  • Failed ho to details correct karke dubara submit kare

Important Tips

  • Official Income Tax portal hi use kare
  • PAN aur Aadhaar details exact match ho
  • Registered mobile number active ho OTP ke liye
  • Successful linking acknowledgement safe rakhe
  • Errors avoid karne ke liye portal instructions follow kare

Benefits of PAN-Aadhaar Linking

  • Official aur government verified
  • Mandatory for ITR filing
  • Fast verification of tax details
  • Reduce errors aur compliance easy
  • No branch visit required

Conclusion

PAN card ko Aadhaar ke sath online link karna ab fast aur simple hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna PAN-Aadhaar official portal se link kar sakte ho aur future me tax aur other services easily use kar sakte ho.
