Gadi Number Se Owner Ka Naam Check Kare

Agar aap kisi vehicle ka owner naam ya RC details check karna chahte ho to ab ye kaam online ho sakta hai. India me government ne vehicle information check karne ke liye online portal provide kiya hai jisse aap gadi number se owner details check kar sakte ho.

Is article me hum aapko batayenge ki **vehicle owner details online kaise check kare**, kaun se apps aur websites use kar sakte hain aur step by step process kya hai.

Vehicle Owner Details Kya Hoti Hai?

Vehicle owner details ka matlab hai kisi vehicle ki registration information. Isme vehicle owner ka naam, vehicle type, registration date aur RC details hoti hain.

  • Owner Name
  • Vehicle Model
  • Registration Date
  • Fuel Type
  • Insurance Status

Vehicle Owner Details Check Karne Ke Liye Kya Chahiye

  • Vehicle Number
  • Internet Connection
  • Mobile Phone ya Computer

Vehicle Owner Details Online Kaise Check Kare

Step 1

Sabse pehle government ki official website open kare:

https://parivahan.gov.in

Step 2

Ab **Know Your Vehicle Details** option par click kare.

Step 3

Vehicle registration number enter kare.

Step 4

Captcha fill kare aur submit button par click kare.

Step 5

Ab screen par vehicle ki details show ho jayegi.

mParivahan App Se Vehicle Details Check Kare

Government ka official app **mParivahan** bhi vehicle details check karne ke liye use kiya ja sakta hai.

Steps:
  • Play Store open kare
  • mParivahan app install kare
  • Vehicle number enter kare
  • Owner details check kare

Vehicle Details Check Karne Ke Benefits
