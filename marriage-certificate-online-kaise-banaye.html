Marriage Certificate Online Kaise Banaye 2026

Complete Step by Step Hinglish Guide

Marriage Certificate ek official document hai jo aapki marriage ko legal aur government recognized proof deta hai. Ye certificate bank, passport, visa, pension aur government schemes ke liye zaruri hai.

Marriage Certificate Kya Hai?

Marriage Certificate ek legal document hai jo proof karta hai ki do individuals legally married hai. India me ye **Registrar of Marriages / Municipal Corporation** ke under issue hota hai.

Marriage Certificate Ke Types

  • Hindu Marriage Certificate: Hindu Marriage Act ke under issue hota hai.
  • Special Marriage Certificate: Agar inter-religion ya civil marriage ho toh Special Marriage Act ke under issue hota hai.
  • Online Marriage Certificate: Kai states me portal ke through apply kiya ja sakta hai.

Marriage Certificate Ke Benefits

  • Legal proof of marriage
  • Passport aur PAN card ke liye support document
  • Government schemes aur pension ke liye mandatory
  • Inheritance aur property ke liye proof
  • Visa aur travel ke liye required

Marriage Certificate Banane Ke Documents

  • Husband aur Wife ke Aadhaar Card
  • Residential proof (Electricity bill, Voter ID, Ration card)
  • Passport size photographs of both partners
  • Marriage invitation card (if available)
  • Age proof (Birth certificate / School certificate / PAN card)
  • Divorce/death certificate if previously married

Marriage Certificate Online Apply Kaise Kare

Step 1 State official marriage registration portal open kare

Har state ka apna portal hota hai. Jaise:

  • Delhi: Delhi e-District
  • Maharashtra: Maharashtra Marriage Registration Portal
  • Karnataka: Karnataka e-District

Step 2 New user registration kare

Portal me email/mobile number se account create kare.

Step 3 Login kare

Registered credentials se login kare.

Step 4 Marriage certificate apply option select kare

Dashboard me “Apply for Marriage Certificate” option select kare.

Step 5 Form fill kare

  • Husband & Wife ke naam
  • Marriage date & place
  • Residential addresses
  • Witness details
  • Registrar name (agar known ho)

Step 6 Documents upload kare

Required documents upload kare jaise Aadhaar, photos, residential proof.

Step 7 Fees pay kare

Online payment available hota hai. Fees ₹100-₹500 depend karta hai state aur portal par.

Step 8 Form submit kare

Submit karne ke baad confirmation aur application number milega. Save kare for future reference.

Step 9 Marriage certificate download kare

Verification ke baad aap PDF download kar sakte ho ya physical copy receive kar sakte ho.

Marriage Certificate Status Kaise Check Kare

  • State portal open kare
  • Application number enter kare
  • Status check kare

Common Problems

  • Incomplete form submission
  • Document mismatch
  • Delay in approval
  • Wrong details in certificate
