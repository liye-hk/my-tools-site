EPF Withdrawal / Claim Status Check Online 2026

Step by Step Official Guide for UAN Members

EPF (Employees’ Provident Fund) withdrawal aur claim status check karna ab online possible hai. Agar aapka PF account active hai aur aapko withdrawal karna hai ya claim ka status check karna hai, ye guide step by step help karegi.

EPF Kya Hai

EPF ek social security scheme hai jisme employees aur employers monthly contribution karte hain. Ye retirement ke liye fund provide karta hai. EPF withdrawal aur claim process ko simplify karne ke liye online portal available hai.

Required Details

  • UAN (Universal Account Number)
  • Registered mobile number linked with UAN
  • Aadhaar number linked with EPF
  • Bank account linked with EPF
  • EPF passbook / previous contributions (optional)

EPF Online Withdrawal / Claim Kaise Kare

Step 1 – UAN Member Portal Open Kare

Browser me EPF UAN Member Portal open kare.

Step 2 – Login Kare

Apna UAN number aur password enter kare. Registered mobile number pe OTP aayega, usse verify kare.

Step 3 – KYC Details Verify Kare

Aadhaar, PAN aur bank details linked hai ya nahi check kare. Agar KYC complete nahi hai, to pehle update kare.

Step 4 – Online Claim Form Select Kare

“Online Services → Claim (Form-31, 19, 10C)” select kare. Form type depend karta hai ki partial withdrawal chahiye ya full EPF surrender / retirement benefit.

Step 5 – Claim Type Choose Kare

  • Form 19 – Final Settlement
  • Form 10C – Pension Withdrawal
  • Form 31 – Partial EPF Withdrawal

Step 6 – Claim Form Fill Kare

Amount, reason for withdrawal, bank account details aur nominee details enter kare. Sabhi details carefully fill kare.

Step 7 – Submit Aur OTP Verify Kare

Submit button click kare aur registered mobile number pe OTP aayega. OTP enter karke claim confirm kare.

EPF Claim Status Online Check Kaise Kare

Step 1 – UAN Portal Login Kare

Portal me login kare apne UAN number aur password se.

Step 2 – Online Services → Track Claim Status

“Online Services → Track Claim Status” option select kare. Yahan aapko recent submitted claims dikhenge.

Step 3 – Claim Reference Number Check Kare

Har submitted claim ke liye reference number diya gaya hai. Status: Pending / Approved / Processed dikhega.

Step 4 – Bank Account Check Kare

Once approved, amount aapke linked bank account me transfer ho jayega.

Important Tips

  • Official EPF portal hi use kare
  • UAN aur Aadhaar linked hone chahiye
  • Claim reference number note kare for future tracking
  • Partial withdrawal ke liye terms aur eligibility check kare
  • Bank account details accurate hone chahiye

Benefits of Online EPF Claim

  • Bank branch jane ki zarurat nahi
  • Fast and secure official process
  • Instant reference number aur tracking
  • 24/7 access from mobile or computer
  • Easy partial or full withdrawal

Conclusion

EPF withdrawal aur claim status check karna ab online safe aur easy hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna EPF fund easily claim kar sakte ho aur status track kar sakte ho.
