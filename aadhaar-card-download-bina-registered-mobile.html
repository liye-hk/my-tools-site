Tip: Apna Aadhaar Number aur Date of Birth ready rakhein. OTP verification ke liye alternate email ya mobile ka use karein.

Step by Step Aadhaar Card Download

  1. 1 Visit kare UIDAI Official Website.
  2. 2 “Download Aadhaar” option select kare.
  3. 3 Form me apna Aadhaar Number, Full Name, Date of Birth enter kare.
  4. 4 “I don’t have registered mobile number” checkbox select kare.
  5. 5 Captcha solve kare aur submit button press kare.
  6. 6 OTP ke liye alternate email/mobile ka use kare aur verify kare.
  7. 7 OTP verify hone ke baad Aadhaar PDF download kare aur save kare.

Important Tips

  • Alternate email/mobile OTP ke liye ready rakhein.
  • Form me details bilkul exact fill karein.
  • Download ke baad soft copy aur print copy dono rakhein.

Common Problems

  • OTP nahi aata? → Email/mobile check kare aur resend OTP try kare.
