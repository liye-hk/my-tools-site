Aadhaar eKYC / Address Update Online 2026

Step by Step Official UIDAI Guide

Aadhaar eKYC aur address update karna ab simple aur official process hai. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi, taaki aap apne Aadhaar ke details easily update ya correct kar sakein online.

Aadhaar eKYC / Address Update Kya Hai

Aadhaar eKYC ka matlab hai ki aap apna Aadhaar number bank, mobile or other services ke liye verify karte ho. Address update ka matlab hai ki aap apne Aadhaar me naya address add ya correct karte ho.

Required Documents / Details

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Registered Mobile Number (OTP verification ke liye)
  • Address Proof (if address update)
  • Email ID (optional for receipt)
  • Scanned copy / photo of supporting documents

Step 1 – UIDAI Official Portal Open Kare

Browser me UIDAI Official Portal open kare. Ye Government of India ka official portal hai.

Step 2 – Aadhaar Self Service Update

“Update Aadhaar / eKYC” section me jaaye aur “Update Address Online” ya “eKYC Update” option select kare.

Step 3 – Login Using Aadhaar Number

Aadhaar number enter kare aur OTP verify kare jo registered mobile number pe aayega. OTP enter karne ke baad next step me proceed kare.

Step 4 – Update / Correct Details

  • Address change / correction select kare
  • Naya address fill kare ya existing details correct kare
  • Scanned supporting document upload kare
  • Submit kare aur acknowledgement receipt download kare

Step 5 – eKYC Update

Bank, mobile ya other services ke liye eKYC update karna ho to “eKYC Update” option select kare aur OTP verify kare. System automatically update confirm karega.

Step 6 – Track Update Status

“Check Update Status” option select kare aur Update Request Number (URN) enter kare. Status – Pending / Processed / Rejected – check kare aur receipt save kare.

Important Tips

  • Official UIDAI portal hi use kare
  • Registered mobile number active ho
  • Scanned documents clear aur valid ho
  • URN safe rakhe future tracking ke liye
  • Multiple updates hai to alag-alag request submit kare

Benefits of Online Aadhaar Update / eKYC

  • Fast and official process without visiting Aadhaar center
  • Secure and verified update
  • Track update status online
  • eKYC instantly updated for bank/mobile services
  • Save time aur hassle-free process

Conclusion

Aadhaar eKYC aur address update online ab simple aur secure process hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apne Aadhaar details easily update / correct kar sakte ho aur official receipt save kar sakte ho.
