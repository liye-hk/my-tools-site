SBI YONO App Me Bank Account Kaise Open Kare 2026

Step by Step Complete Hinglish Guide

SBI YONO app ke zariye ab aap ghar baithe hi bank account open kar sakte ho. Is guide me aapko step by step process, required documents, KYC process aur account activate karne ka detailed guide milega.

SBI YONO App Kya Hai?

SBI YONO (You Only Need One) ek digital banking platform hai jisse aap SBI ke saare banking products use kar sakte ho. Isme aap bank account open kar sakte ho, loans apply kar sakte ho, investments aur insurance manage kar sakte ho.

Benefits of Opening SBI Bank Account via YONO App

  • Ghar baithe account open karna
  • Zero balance account options
  • Instant debit card aur net banking facility
  • Digital fund transfer aur bill payment
  • Secure aur fast KYC process

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card (Identity proof)
  • Pan Card (Tax ID)
  • Residential Proof (Ration card, electricity bill, voter ID)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Email aur mobile number (registered)

Step by Step SBI YONO App Account Opening Process

Step 1 YONO App Install Kare

  • App store ya Google Play store se SBI YONO App download kare.

Step 2 New User Registration Kare

App open kare, “New User Registration” option select kare aur apna mobile number enter kare. OTP verify kare.

Step 3 Login Kare

Registered credentials se login kare aur “Open Savings Account” option select kare.

Step 4 Account Type Select Kare

Basic savings account ya regular account choose kare. Zero balance account bhi option me hoga.

Step 5 Personal Details Fill Kare

  • Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Residential address
  • Nominee details (optional)

Step 6 Documents Upload Kare

Scan aur upload kare Aadhaar, Pan card aur residential proof.

Step 7 Video KYC Verification

SBI ek short video call karega aapke saath for KYC verification.

Step 8 Form Submit Kare

Form submit karne ke baad aapko application reference number milega.

Step 9 Account Activate Kare

Verification complete hone ke baad account activate ho jayega aur debit card aur net banking details app me show honge.

Zero Balance Account Options in SBI YONO

  • SBI Basic Savings Account – Zero balance
  • SBI Digital Savings Account – Instant debit card
  • SBI YONO Lite Account – Limited online transactions

Common Issues & Solutions

  • OTP Not Received – Check registered mobile and network
  • Document Mismatch – Upload correct documents
  • Video KYC Not Connecting – Stable internet aur proper lighting
  • Account Activation Delay – Contact SBI Customer Care
