DigiLocker Se Aadhaar & Certificates Download Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Official Guide

DigiLocker Government of India ka official digital platform hai jahan se aap apne Aadhaar, birth/death certificate, school/college certificate aur other government certificates online download kar sakte ho. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi.

DigiLocker Kya Hai

DigiLocker ek secure cloud-based platform hai jahan aap apne government documents digitally access, store aur share kar sakte ho. Ye service Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY) ke under hai.

Required Details

  • Registered mobile number linked with Aadhaar
  • Aadhaar number
  • Email ID (optional)
  • Documents ke liye reference number (agar apply kiye ho previously)

DigiLocker Me Account Kaise Banaye

Step 1 – Official DigiLocker Portal Open Kare

Browser me DigiLocker Official Website open kare ya DigiLocker app install kare (Android/iOS).

Step 2 – Sign Up / Login Kare

Sign up ke liye Aadhaar number enter kare aur OTP verify kare. Agar already account hai to login kare.

Step 3 – Dashboard Explore Kare

Dashboard me “Issued Documents” aur “Uploaded Documents” sections hote hain. Yahan se certificates access kar sakte ho.

Aadhaar & Certificates Download Kaise Kare

Step 1 – Aadhaar Link Kare

“Issued Documents → Add Document → Aadhaar” select kare aur link kare. OTP verify hoga registered mobile number par.

Step 2 – Certificates Search Kare

“Issued Documents → Ministry / State Board” select kare. Example: Birth Certificate, School Certificate, Income Certificate etc.

Step 3 – Download / Save PDF

Required certificate select kare aur “Download” button click kare. PDF apne device me save kar sakte ho ya print kar sakte ho.

Step 4 – Share Securely

Documents share karne ke liye “Share” button use kare. Receiver ke paas digitally verifyable copy jayegi.

Important Tips

  • Official DigiLocker portal/app hi use kare
  • OTP aur Aadhaar details safe rakhe
  • Certificates digital form me hi verify kare, print optional
  • Document expiry date check kare
  • Government issued certificates hamesha verifyable hoti hain

Benefits of DigiLocker

  • 24/7 access to government documents
  • Physical document carry karne ki zarurat nahi
  • Safe, secure, and government verified
  • Instant download & share facility

Conclusion

DigiLocker se Aadhaar aur other certificates download karna safe, fast aur official process hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apne required certificates easily download aur share kar sakte ho.
