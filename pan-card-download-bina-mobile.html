PAN Card Download Without Registered Mobile Number 2026

Agar aap apna PAN Card online download karna chahte hain lekin aapka mobile number registered nahi hai, to tension lene ki zarurat nahi hai. Ye guide step by step batayegi ki kaise aap bina registered mobile number ke PAN card download kar sakte hain.

Important: Apna PAN number aur date of birth ready rakhe. Aadhaar linked email ka use kar sakte ho.

Step by Step PAN Card Download

  1. 1 Visit kare NSDL PAN Service website.
  2. 2 Click kare “Download PAN” option par.
  3. 3 Form me apna PAN number, full name aur date of birth enter kare.
  4. 4 “I don’t have registered mobile number” option select kare.
  5. 5 Apna Aadhaar linked mobile/email verify kare.
  6. 6 OTP ya verification code enter kare aur submit kare.
  7. 7 PAN card ka PDF download kare aur safe jagah save kare.

Important Tips

  • Aadhaar linked email/mobile ka use kare.
  • Safe aur secure environment me download kare.
  • Soft copy aur printed copy dono rakhe.
  • Form me details exactly PAN card ke sath match karna chahiye.

Common Problems

  • OTP nahi aaya? → Aadhaar linked mobile/email check kare.
  • Form details mismatch? → PAN details dobara check kare.
  • Website slow hai? → Thodi der baad try kare.
