e-Shram Card Kya Hai?

e-Shram Card ek **Government of India initiative** hai jo Ministry of Labour & Employment ne launch kiya hai. Iska main purpose hai India ke unorganized workers ka **national database create karna**.

Is card me worker ko ek **12 digit Universal Account Number (UAN)** diya jata hai jo lifetime valid hota hai.

Official portal: https://eshram.gov.in
