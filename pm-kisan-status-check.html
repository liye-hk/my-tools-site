PM Kisan Status Check Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Guide in Hinglish

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana India ki sabse popular farmer schemes me se ek hai. Is scheme ke through government farmers ko har saal ₹6000 ki financial assistance deti hai. Ye amount 3 installments me directly bank account me transfer kiya jata hai.

Bahut se farmers ko pata nahi hota ki unka PM Kisan payment status kya hai ya next installment kab milega. Is article me aapko step by step process bataya gaya hai jisse aap easily PM Kisan status online check kar sakte ho.

PM Kisan Yojana Kya Hai?

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi ek government scheme hai jo small aur marginal farmers ko financial support provide karti hai. Is scheme ke under eligible farmers ko har saal ₹6000 diya jata hai.

  • ₹6000 per year financial help
  • 3 installments of ₹2000
  • Direct Bank Transfer (DBT)
  • Government funded scheme

PM Kisan Status Check Karne Ke Liye Required Details

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Mobile Number
  • Registration Number
  • Internet connection

Step by Step PM Kisan Status Check Online

Step 1 Official Website Open Kare

Sabse pehle PM Kisan ki official website open kare:

https://pmkisan.gov.in

Step 2 Beneficiary Status Option Select Kare

Website open karne ke baad homepage par “Beneficiary Status” option dikhai dega. Is option par click kare.

Step 3 Details Enter Kare

Ab aapko status check karne ke liye details enter karni hogi:

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Mobile Number
  • Registration Number

Step 4 OTP Verification Kare

Registered mobile number par OTP aayega. OTP enter karke verify kare.

Step 5 Payment Status Check Kare

Verification ke baad screen par aapko complete details dikhegi:

  • Installment payment status
  • Next installment date
  • Bank account details
  • Application status

PM Kisan Installment List Kaise Check Kare

Farmers PM Kisan beneficiary list bhi online check kar sakte hain.

Steps:

  • Official website open kare
  • Farmer Corner option select kare
  • Beneficiary List par click kare
  • State, district aur village select kare
  • List download kare

PM Kisan Payment Nahi Aaya To Kya Kare?

Agar aapko PM Kisan payment nahi mila hai to ye reasons ho sakte hain:

  • Aadhaar bank se link nahi hai
  • KYC incomplete hai
  • Bank details incorrect hai
  • Application verification pending hai

Aap apna KYC update kar sakte ho:

PM Kisan eKYC Update

PM Kisan eKYC Kaise Kare

  • Official website open kare
  • eKYC option par click kare
  • Aadhaar number enter kare
  • OTP verify kare
  • KYC complete ho jayega
